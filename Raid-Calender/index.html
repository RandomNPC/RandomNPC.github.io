About

Welcome User!

This is the Legendary Grounds Calendar; The event planner for our full clan raids.

Clan members and friends gather every two days on runescape to slay the Mazcab bosses, Beastmaster and Yakamaru. An event is hosted by one of our clan members and posted using this web application. Simply sign in with your google account, select events you want to attend, then show up at raid time.
